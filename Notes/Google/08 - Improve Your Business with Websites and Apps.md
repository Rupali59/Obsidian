## 1. Planning Your Website

### Why a Compelling Website Matters

A strong website builds trust, connects with your audience, and drives results. It's a key tool to inspire action and support your goals.

### Aligning Goals with Audience Needs

When creating your website, combine your business goals with what visitors want. Use the SMART framework from [[02 - Build Your Digital Marketing Strategy#SMART Goals Framework]] to make goals clear, actionable, and trackable.

| Goal | Audience Needs | Approach |
|------|----------------|----------|
| **Increase online sales** | Product details they can trust; easy checkout | Product stories, customer reviews, clear CTAs |
| **Generate more leads** | Helpful info; simple way to stay updated | Newsletter sign-up, valuable content, lead magnets |
| **Build brand awareness** | Interesting, relatable content | Blog, engaging articles, 'About' page with your story |
| **Increase foot traffic** | Convenience; incentives | Maps, directions, in-store events, promotions |
| **Drive phone calls** | Easy contact options | Prominent phone number; for mobile, one-tap call button |
| **Enhance customer support** | Easy access to help | Support page: FAQs, contact options, AI chatbot |

### Using AI to Understand Audience Needs

AI tools can analyse social media, competitor activity, and trends to uncover what interests your audience. Use ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini to brainstorm site structure, generate copy drafts, or analyse competitor websites. For the T-C-R-E-I prompting framework, see [[04 - Create Engaging Content#3. Using AI for Content Creation]].

## 2. Structuring Your Website

Make it easy for customers to find what they need. A well-structured site keeps visitors engaged and reduces frustration. See [[04 - Create Engaging Content]] for planning and creating content.

### Common Page Types

| Page | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| **Home** | Hero section (image/video), key offerings, testimonials, clear CTAs. Engage immediately. |
| **Services/Products** | Descriptions, pricing, testimonials; product images, descriptions, purchase options. |
| **Blog/News** | Fresh content for engagement and SEO. Industry trends, company updates, tips. |
| **Contact** | Address, phone, email, map, contact form, social links. |
| **About** | Your story, team, values, mission, photos of your work. |
| **FAQ** | Common questions to reduce enquiries and improve experience. Schema markup (FAQPage) helps these appear in search. |

## 3. Developing Your Website

### Development Approaches

| Approach | Best For | Notes |
|----------|----------|-------|
| **Website builder** | Beginners | Budget-friendly, drag-and-drop, ready-made templates, no coding. Wix, Squarespace, Carrd. |
| **CMS** | Flexibility | Content management system; more customisation; scale as you grow. WordPress, Webflow. |
| **No-code / low-code** | Custom without coding | Webflow, Framer—design-led, powerful without traditional development. |
| **Hire a professional** | Custom solutions | Freelancer or agency for tailored site; requires budget. |

### Hosting Options

| Option | Use Case |
|--------|----------|
| **Cloud hosting** | Larger or complex sites; handles high traffic; distributes across multiple servers. Vercel, Netlify, AWS, Cloudflare. |
| **Built-in hosting** | Website builders and CMS often include hosting; budget-friendly for smaller sites. |

### Website Design (UX)

| Principle | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Intuitive navigation** | Logical menu, clear labels, familiar icons. Users know where to go. |
| **Consistency** | Same colours, fonts, interactions across pages. Familiar feel. |
| **Reduce friction** | Fewer steps to complete actions; CTAs in visible spots; key info a few clicks away. |
| **Function before style** | Usability first: clear menus, readable text, simple layouts. |
| **Mobile-first** | Design for mobile screens first, then scale up to desktop. |

### Speed, Performance, and Core Web Vitals

| Metric | What It Measures | Target |
|--------|------------------|--------|
| **LCP (Largest Contentful Paint)** | Loading speed of main content | ≤ 2.5 seconds |
| **INP (Interaction to Next Paint)** | Responsiveness to user input | ≤ 200 milliseconds |
| **CLS (Cumulative Layout Shift)** | Visual stability (no unexpected jumps) | ≤ 0.1 |

Test with Google PageSpeed Insights. Compress images, use modern formats (WebP, AVIF), limit large files, choose reliable hosting with CDN and caching.

### Website Accessibility

Designing for everyone improves UX and helps meet legal requirements:

| Requirement | Action |
|-------------|--------|
| **Alt text** | Descriptive text for all images |
| **Contrast** | Sufficient colour contrast for readability |
| **Font size** | 16px minimum for body text |
| **Keyboard navigation** | All interactive elements reachable via keyboard |
| **Screen reader support** | Semantic HTML, ARIA labels where needed |
| **Captions/transcripts** | For video and audio content |
| **Testing** | Use Lighthouse, axe DevTools, or WAVE to audit |

## 4. Mobile Apps

### Benefits for Businesses

| Benefit | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Improved engagement** | Direct connection: push notifications, support messages, tailored content. |
| **Increased loyalty** | Loyalty programmes: earn points, redeem rewards, track progress. |
| **Simplified actions** | Quick checkout; biometric login (fingerprint, Face ID). |
| **Better insights** | Analytics show user behaviour, feature usage, conversion paths. |

### Development Options

| Option | Best For | Notes |
|--------|----------|-------|
| **Native apps** | Best performance | Platform-specific (iOS/Android). Full access to device features. Requires largest investment. |
| **Cross-platform** | Efficiency | One codebase for both platforms (React Native, Flutter). Good balance of performance and cost. |
| **App builder platform** | No coding | Templates, easy tools (Glide, Adalo); may limit customisation. |
| **Progressive Web Apps (PWAs)** | Cost-effective alternative | App-like experience in browser; no download; offline access, push notifications. No app store approval needed. |

## 5. AI Chatbots and Conversational AI

### Benefits of AI Chatbots

| Benefit | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Instant responses** | Faster service; customers get help without delay. |
| **24/7 availability** | Support outside regular hours across time zones. |
| **Reduced workload** | Handle routine questions; free teams for complex tasks. |
| **Personalisation** | AI-powered chatbots learn from interactions and can personalise responses. |
| **Lead qualification** | Chatbots can capture information and qualify leads before handoff to sales. |

### Modern Chatbot Options

| Type | Description | Examples |
|------|-------------|---------|
| **Rule-based** | Pre-defined flows and decision trees. Good for FAQs and simple tasks. | Tidio, ManyChat |
| **AI-powered** | Uses large language models to understand intent and generate responses. Handles nuanced questions. | Intercom Fin, Drift, Zendesk AI, ChatGPT-powered bots |
| **Hybrid** | Starts with AI; escalates to human agents for complex issues. | Intercom, Freshdesk |

### Implementation Steps

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| **1. Identify tasks** | Define what the chatbot will handle: FAQs, orders, troubleshooting, lead capture. |
| **2. Choose platform** | Match complexity to tool. Rule-based for simple flows; AI-powered for natural conversation. |
| **3. Train and configure** | Feed knowledge base, FAQs, product information. Set escalation rules. |
| **4. Test and iterate** | Monitor conversations, identify gaps, refine responses. |

## 6. Checklists

### Website Planning Checklist

- [ ] List content you'd share based on goals or interests
- [ ] Organise items into categories (e.g. Products, About Us, FAQs)
- [ ] Use AI to brainstorm structure recommendations

### Website Design Checklist

- [ ] Review other sites: style, colours, layout
- [ ] Sketch rough layout for homepage (text, images, CTAs)
- [ ] Test Core Web Vitals with PageSpeed Insights
- [ ] Verify mobile responsiveness

### Accessibility Checklist

- [ ] Test with Lighthouse or axe DevTools
- [ ] Verify keyboard navigation works for all interactive elements
- [ ] Check colour contrast meets WCAG 2.1 AA standards
- [ ] Ensure all images have alt text

### Mobile App Decision Checklist

- [ ] Explore how similar businesses use mobile apps
- [ ] Consider whether a PWA could meet your needs at lower cost
- [ ] Identify simple tasks customers could do via app (browse products, check order status)
- [ ] Research development options for your business type and budget

### Chatbot Checklist

- [ ] List top 10 questions your support team receives
- [ ] Decide: rule-based, AI-powered, or hybrid?
- [ ] Define escalation triggers (when to hand off to a human)
- [ ] Plan knowledge base content to train the chatbot
