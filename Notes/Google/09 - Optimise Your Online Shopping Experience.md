## 1. Using E-commerce to Sell

### Why E-commerce?

E-commerce opens up growth possibilities for many businesses—whether a local store expanding online or a large retailer reaching new customers.

### What is E-commerce?

Selling things online through websites, online marketplaces, mobile apps, and social media. Done well, it narrows the gap between retail and online shopping.

### E-commerce Aims

| Aim | Description |
|-----|-------------|
| **Payments** | Let customers pay through the web or an app. |
| **Inventory** | Let people view and search your inventory on a website or app. |
| **Accounts & subscriptions** | Let customers create accounts and set up recurring orders and subscriptions. |

### Getting Started with E-commerce

| Option | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Payment services** | Add payment options to your site. Support credit cards, direct debit, digital wallets (Google Pay, Apple Pay, Shop Pay). Customers can pay securely without an account. |
| **Off-the-shelf solutions** | All-in-one e-commerce platforms (Shopify, WooCommerce, Squarespace, BigCommerce) with templates. Features: product search, inventory management, checkout, customer accounts, order management. |
| **Online catalogue** | Show products online as you would in-store. Photos, detailed descriptions, reviews, videos. High-quality imagery and content are your online display. |
| **Marketplace selling** | List on established platforms (Amazon, Etsy, eBay) to access their existing traffic and trust. Lower setup cost; higher per-sale fees. |

## 2. Taking Payments and Managing Orders

### Payment Options

| Option | Best For | Notes |
|--------|----------|-------|
| **Third-party payment processing** | Quick setup | Customer goes to a hosted checkout (Stripe, PayPal, Square); transaction handled for you. Focus on the business; provider manages security and PCI compliance. |
| **Integrated payments** | Direct checkout | Embed payment directly in your site; customers pay without leaving. Stripe Elements, Shopify Payments, and similar tools handle this. |
| **Full e-commerce solution** | Growing businesses | Larger inventories, customisation, broader reach. Backend system with order management. |
| **Buy Now, Pay Later (BNPL)** | Reducing cart abandonment | Services like Klarna, Afterpay, and Clearpay let customers split payments. Can increase average order value. |

### Full E-commerce Backend Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Customer management** | Accounts; personal info, billing, shipping addresses; reviews. AI-powered segmentation for personalised offers. |
| **Payments** | Advanced control; PCI-compliant security; fraud detection (Stripe Radar, Shopify Protect); high transaction volumes. |
| **Inventory** | Upload products; organise categories and product pages; real-time stock syncing across channels. |
| **Order fulfilment** | Order management; shipping tracking; integration with accounting and logistics systems. |

## 3. Creating a Smooth E-commerce Experience

### Be Your Own Customer

Imagine you're a first-time visitor. Rate how easy the experience feels. Ask a friend to review for fresh insights.

**Impact of a difficult experience:** Customers may spend less, not buy, have a negative perception, or not recommend your business.

### Improving E-commerce with Analytics

Use GA4 (Google Analytics 4) e-commerce tracking to measure the full purchase funnel.

| Area | Improvement |
|------|-------------|
| **Device optimisation** | Many visits from mobile but few purchases? Implement responsive design. Test checkout on mobile specifically—thumb-friendly buttons, auto-fill, minimal form fields. |
| **Navigation & search** | Easy categories and subcategories (e.g. rooms → furniture types). Add a search function with filters. AI-powered search (Algolia, Searchspring) improves product discovery. |
| **Product pages** | Visits but no adds to cart? Add video, 360° views, galleries, quality photos, detailed descriptions, measurements. AR try-on tools (for fashion, furniture) reduce returns. |
| **Customer accounts** | Cart abandonment? Allow storing shipping, billing, payment preferences. Use past purchases, searches, and views to recommend products. Reward loyal customers with tailored offers. |
| **Checkout optimisation** | Guest checkout option, progress indicators, multiple payment methods, auto-address lookup, trust badges. One-click checkout (Shop Pay, Amazon Pay) reduces friction. |

## 4. Product Promotion and Merchandising

### Getting Products Noticed

Create a clear hierarchy of categories and subcategories. Easier navigation makes it more likely customers will purchase.

### Product Merchandising

| Approach | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **Offline example** | Featured products (e.g. desks, bookcases for back-to-school). |
| **Online** | Feature key items on the homepage—clear out older stock or highlight best-sellers. Use urgency cues ("Only 3 left", "Selling fast"). |

### Data-Driven Promotions

| Technique | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **AI-powered recommendations** | Machine learning analyses browsing and purchase patterns to suggest products. Most platforms (Shopify, WooCommerce, BigCommerce) include this or offer plugins. |
| **Recommended products** | Show related items after viewing (e.g. chairs when viewing tables). AI personalisation engines (Dynamic Yield, Nosto) tailor recommendations per visitor. |
| **Related products** | Cross-sell by showing items customers might not search for (e.g. desk + matching bookcases, chairs). |
| **Personalised offers** | Use first-party data to send targeted discounts based on browsing history, cart contents, and purchase frequency. |

> **Retargeting:** For retargeting strategies (remarketing ads, cart abandonment campaigns, frequency capping, and privacy considerations), see [[11 - Choose the Right Marketing Channels for Your Budget#4. Retargeting]].

## 5. Social Commerce and Live Shopping

### Selling Directly on Social Platforms

Social commerce lets customers browse and buy without leaving the platform. Most major platforms now support in-app checkout.

| Platform | Commerce Features |
|----------|-------------------|
| **Instagram Shopping** | Product tags in posts/Reels/Stories, in-app checkout, shoppable collections |
| **TikTok Shop** | In-video product links, live shopping, affiliate marketplace |
| **Facebook Shops** | Customisable storefront, catalogue sync, Messenger-based support |
| **Pinterest Shopping** | Product pins, visual search to buy, catalogue integration |
| **YouTube Shopping** | Product shelves, tagged products in videos, live shopping events |

### Live Shopping

Real-time video streams where hosts demonstrate products and viewers purchase instantly. Combines entertainment with shopping—high engagement and conversion rates.

| Element | Best Practice |
|---------|---------------|
| **Format** | Product demos, Q&A, exclusive launches, behind-the-scenes |
| **Engagement** | Respond to comments live; create urgency with limited-time offers |
| **Platform** | TikTok Live, Instagram Live, YouTube Live, dedicated platforms (Bambuser, CommentSold) |

## 6. Checklists

### E-commerce Goals Checklist

- [ ] Revisit strategy, goals, and persona from [[02 - Build Your Digital Marketing Strategy]] and [[03 - Know Your Digital Customers]]
- [ ] Identify an e-commerce aim that supports your strategy
- [ ] Set 2–3 SMART goals that support your e-commerce aim

### Payments and Orders Checklist

- [ ] Revisit e-commerce aim and goals from the Using E-commerce lesson
- [ ] Decide: third-party processing, integrated payments, or full e-commerce system
- [ ] Evaluate BNPL options (Klarna, Afterpay) for your audience
- [ ] Compare three options and their key selling points

### Promotion and Merchandising Checklist

- [ ] Visit 3 competitor websites
- [ ] Note items featured on their homepage
- [ ] Check if they use AI-powered product recommendations
- [ ] Reflect on what seems effective and why

### Social Commerce Checklist

- [ ] Identify which platforms your customers already shop on
- [ ] Set up product catalogues on 1–2 priority platforms
- [ ] Test a live shopping session and measure engagement vs. standard posts
