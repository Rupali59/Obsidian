## 1. Customer Personas

### Segmentation

Dividing a broad audience into smaller groups based on demographics, location, interests, behaviours, and needs.

| Type | Focus | Example |
|------|-------|---------|
| **Demographic** | Age, gender, education, occupation | Travel agency targeting under-30s holidays |
| **Geographic** | City, region, country | E-commerce promoting products only where delivery is available |
| **Psychographic** | Values, interests, lifestyle | Camping gear targeting adventurous, sustainability-minded travellers |
| **Behavioural** | Purchasing habits, product usage, online activity | Gym offering discounts to frequent vs. occasional members |

### Benefits of Segmentation

| Benefit | Outcome |
|---------|---------|
| Save time and resources | Focus on high-potential groups |
| Personalise messages | Tailored content increases relevance and engagement |
| Improve advertising | Precise targeting; supports GDPR compliance |
| Drive engagement | Right message, right people, right time |

### AI-Powered Persona Creation

AI tools (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini) can accelerate persona creation by analysing customer data, reviews, and competitor audiences. Feed in your customer data or describe your business, and AI can draft persona profiles, suggest segmentation approaches, and identify gaps in your understanding. Always validate AI-generated personas against real customer feedback.

### Persona Template

| Element | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Who** | Name, age, location, occupation, household |
| **Goal** | What they want to achieve through your product or service |
| **Barrier** | Challenges preventing them from achieving that goal |
| **Digital habits** | Preferred platforms, content formats, time online, devices used |
| **Campaign message** | Message addressing the barrier with a clear solution |

## 2. Customer Buying Behaviours

### Shopping Channels

| Channel | Characteristics |
|---------|------------------|
| **Offline** | Physical interaction, staff assistance, in-store displays |
| **Online** | Descriptions, photos, reviews, comparison tools; wider range, convenience |
| **Social commerce** | Browse and buy directly within platforms (Instagram Shopping, TikTok Shop) |
| **Omnichannel** | Seamless experience across channels—try in-store, buy online (or vice versa); consistent branding and pricing |

### See, Think, Do, Care Framework

| Stage | Focus | Example |
|-------|-------|---------|
| **See** | Awareness through content or social interactions | Influencer shares running gear → customer feels inspired |
| **Think** | Research solutions; explore options, read reviews, compare prices | Searches "best running shoes for beginners", reads reviews, asks AI assistant |
| **Do** | Purchase decision; simple checkout, clear pricing | — |
| **Care** | Post-purchase: reviews, support, loyalty | Shares new shoes on social media, leaves review |

### Factors Influencing Purchase (Do Stage)

| Factor | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Four Ps** | Product, Place, Price, Promotion |
| **Right time** | Availability when needed; seasonal alignment; special offers |
| **Risk reduction** | Warranties, guarantees, return policies |
| **Emotional connection** | Storytelling, community, nostalgia |
| **Convenience** | Fast shipping, easy returns, flexible payment (including BNPL) |
| **Social influence** | Recommendations, reviews, influencer feedback |
| **Sustainability** | Ethical, environmentally friendly practices |
| **Values** | Brand alignment with customer values |
| **Omnichannel** | Seamless online/offline experience |
| **Customer support** | Accessible, responsive, quick resolutions—including AI chatbots |
| **Personalisation** | Tailored recommendations and offers powered by AI and first-party data |

## 3. Mapping the Customer Journey

A customer journey map outlines key touchpoints from discovery to post-purchase engagement.

### Touchpoints

| Type | Examples |
|------|----------|
| **Offline** | Receipts, in-store signage, staff interactions, service counters |
| **Online** | Ads, social media posts, email newsletters, website interactions |
| **AI-assisted** | Chatbot conversations, AI assistant recommendations, voice search results |

### Stages, Focus & Goals

| Stage | Focus | Goal |
|-------|-------|------|
| **1. Awareness** | Discover what makes your brand unique | Introduce brand via social, ads, influencers, AI Overviews; reach right audience |
| **2. Engagement** | Build trust with meaningful content | Provide reviews, blogs, videos that help customers decide |
| **3. Conversion** | Make action easy | Minimise barriers; clear descriptions, simple checkout; progress bars, discounts |
| **4. Retention** | Maintain relationships | Follow-up emails, loyalty programmes; turn customers into advocates |

### Key Questions

| Question | Considerations |
|----------|----------------|
| Where do customers search for information? | Search, FAQs, forums, AI assistants, video, Reddit |
| Where do they discover new brands? | Social, email, ads, influencer content, AI recommendations |
| What influences purchase decisions? | Reviews, comparisons, pricing, peers, AI-generated summaries |
| How do they stay engaged after purchase? | Emails, loyalty, community, exclusive content |

### Optimising Touchpoints

| Action | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Monitor performance | Track each touchpoint in GA4; set baselines |
| Adapt to audiences | Adjust messaging when demographics shift |
| Gather feedback | Surveys, reviews; offer multiple feedback methods |
| Stay consistent | Same care and communication across all channels |
| Find bottlenecks | Use funnel analysis to focus efforts where you create greatest impact |

## 4. Selecting Digital Marketing Channels

### Channel Types & Categories

| Category | Description | Examples |
|----------|-------------|----------|
| **Owned** | Channels you control | Website, email lists, social profiles, mobile app |
| **Earned** | From positive customer experiences | Reviews, media coverage, organic shares, UGC |
| **Paid** | Advertising | PPC ads, sponsored posts, display ads, influencer partnerships |

**Types:** Social media, websites, email, search engines, online ads, mobile apps, AI assistants.

### Selection Criteria

| Criterion | Key Questions |
|-----------|---------------|
| Marketing goals | Do channels support goals? (e.g. brand awareness → social, search, video) |
| Customer persona | Where does your audience spend time online? |
| Cost | Upfront and ongoing; social ads vs email tools vs AI automation |
| Ease of use | Learning curve; management effort; AI-assisted scheduling available? |
| Digital presence audit | Which platforms perform well? Where are gaps? |
| Competitors | Which channels do they use? What gaps can you fill? |

## 5. Digital Marketing Strategy Summary

Use goals, personas, customer journeys, and competitive insights to align strategy with business needs.

| Step | Focus | Question |
|------|-------|----------|
| 1 | Marketing goals | What objectives? How do they align with business goals? |
| 2 | Mission and USP | What makes you unique? How do they resonate with customer values? |
| 3 | Digital presence audit | Where performing well? Where are gaps? What do AI assistants say about you? |
| 4 | Competitive landscape | Competitors' strengths/weaknesses? How will you differentiate? |
| 5 | Customer personas | Which persona? What needs can your brand address? |
| 6 | Customer journey | Most impactful touchpoints at each stage? How influence decisions? |
| 7 | Digital channels | Which channels connect with audience and achieve goals? |

## 7. Checklists

### Persona Checklist

- [ ] **Who:** Name, age, location, household, education
- [ ] **Goals:** What does this persona want to achieve?
- [ ] **Barriers:** What prevents them from reaching their goals?
- [ ] **Digital habits:** Where do they spend time online? What devices?

### Journey Checklist

- [ ] **See:** Platform that first captured their attention
- [ ] **Think:** How they explored their options (search, AI, social, reviews)
- [ ] **Do:** What drove them to complete the purchase
- [ ] **Care:** How they engaged with your brand after buying

### Channel Selection Checklist

- [ ] Review customer persona. Which channels fit their habits?
- [ ] Revisit marketing goals (brand awareness, lead generation)
- [ ] Pick three channels that align with persona and goal
- [ ] Assess cost and ease of use. Business account or training needed?
- [ ] Check competitors. Any gaps you can fill?
