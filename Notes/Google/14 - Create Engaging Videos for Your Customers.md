## 1. Video and Your Digital Marketing Strategy

### What is Video Marketing?

Using videos to promote products, services, or your brand. Video engages audiences in a dynamic, memorable way. Short-form video (Reels, Shorts, TikToks) is now the highest-engagement content format across platforms.

### Key Benefits

| Use | Example |
|-----|---------|
| **Campaign video** | Brand awareness |
| **Tutorial** | How-to on YouTube |
| **Live stream** | Product launch on TikTok, Instagram, YouTube |
| **Short-form clip** | Behind-the-scenes on Reels, TikTok, Shorts |
| **UGC** | Customer testimonials, unboxing |

Video helps tell your story, showcase expertise, and engage. Align video with [[02 - Build Your Digital Marketing Strategy]] goals.

### Aligning Video with Goals

| Goal | Approach |
|------|----------|
| **Showcase expertise** | Create tutorials, how-tos, educational content |
| **Attract attention** | Short-form content that drives traffic to site/store/social |
| **Build community** | Live streams, Q&A, behind-the-scenes |
| **Reach without creating** | Buy ad space on relevant videos. See [[11 - Choose the Right Marketing Channels for Your Budget#6. Advertising on Video Platforms]]. |

## 2. Creating Video Content

### Video Formats

| Format | Best For |
|--------|----------|
| **Short-form (<60s)** | Quick tips, product features, trends, sale announcements. TikTok, Reels, Shorts. |
| **Long-form (2+ min)** | Storytelling, tutorials, demos, reviews. YouTube, website. |
| **Live** | Q&A, launches, behind-the-scenes, live shopping. |
| **Tutorial** | How products work; educate on website/YouTube. |
| **Testimonials/UGC** | Customer experiences; trust and authenticity. |
| **Vertical video** | Full-screen mobile-first. Required for Reels, TikTok, Shorts. |

### Planning

| Step | Focus |
|------|-------|
| **Define objectives** | Align with business values and marketing goals. |
| **Generate ideas** | Demos, BTS, contests, challenges, trending audio. AI tools (ChatGPT, Claude) can brainstorm concepts. |
| **Plan efficiently** | Shoot multiple videos in one session. Batch content creation. |
| **Storyboard** | Outline key actions and dialogue. Even a rough phone-notes outline helps. |
| **Adapt per platform** | YouTube = more polished; TikTok/Reel = quick, casual, trending; Live = minimal prep, clear outline. |

### Production

Use locations you have (home, office, shop). Creative angles and lighting make everyday spots work. Smartphone video quality is now professional-grade.

### AI Video Tools

| Tool | Use |
|------|-----|
| **CapCut** | Free editing with AI effects, captions, templates. Integrated with TikTok. |
| **Descript** | Edit video by editing text transcript. AI filler-word removal, green screen. |
| **Runway** | AI video generation, background removal, motion tracking. |
| **Synthesia** | AI-generated presenter videos from text scripts. Good for training, explainers. |
| **Opus Clip** | AI repurposing—turns long videos into short clips automatically. |
| **Auto-captions** | Built into TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, CapCut. Always use them—most viewers watch without sound. |

## 3. Sharing and Promoting Video Content

### Platform Strategy

| Platform | Format | Audience |
|----------|--------|----------|
| **YouTube** | Long-form + Shorts | Broad; tutorial and search-driven; strong SEO value |
| **TikTok** | Short-form + Live | Younger demographics; trend-driven; high discoverability |
| **Instagram** | Reels + Stories | Visual-first; product-driven; influencer ecosystem |
| **LinkedIn** | Native video | B2B; professional insights; thought leadership |
| **Facebook** | Video + Live | Community; events; older demographics |
| **Pinterest** | Idea Pins (video) | Discovery; inspiration; shopping intent |

### Social Media Promotion

| Tactic | Notes |
|--------|-------|
| **Upload natively** | Higher engagement than external links on every platform |
| **Tailor per platform** | Different aspect ratios, styles, captions. Vertical for Reels/TikTok/Shorts; horizontal for YouTube. |
| **Captions always** | 80%+ of social video is watched without sound. Auto-captions or burned-in. |
| **Hashtags** | Research on-platform; mix broad and niche |
| **Encourage interaction** | Questions, comments, shares; quizzes, polls, shoppable links |
| **Repurpose** | One long video → multiple Shorts/Reels/TikToks. Use tools like Opus Clip. |

### Email Marketing

Use email to promote videos: clickable thumbnails (GIF or static), strong copy, targeted segments. See [[13 - Craft Effective Email Campaigns]].

## 4. Measuring Video Performance

### Key Metrics

| Metric | What It Shows |
|--------|---------------|
| **Views / reach** | How many people saw the video |
| **Watch time** | Total minutes watched—YouTube's primary ranking signal |
| **Average view duration** | How much of the video people watch before leaving |
| **Engagement** | Likes, comments, shares, saves |
| **CTR (thumbnail)** | How compelling your thumbnail and title are |
| **Conversion** | Actions taken after watching (site visits, purchases) |
| **Audience** | Age, gender, location—tailor future content |

### Platform Analytics

| Platform | Built-in Analytics |
|----------|-------------------|
| **YouTube Studio** | Watch time, audience retention graph, traffic sources, revenue |
| **TikTok Analytics** | Views, profile visits, follower growth, trending content |
| **Instagram Insights** | Reach, plays, saves, shares, follower demographics |
| **GA4** | Traffic from video platforms, on-site behaviour after video click |

## 5. Checklists

### Video Strategy

- [ ] Reference goals from [[02 - Build Your Digital Marketing Strategy]]
- [ ] Decide: produce content, repurpose existing content, or advertise on others' videos
- [ ] Choose primary platform(s) based on audience

### Content Plan

- [ ] Pick a theme for your business
- [ ] List three video ideas
- [ ] Decide format per idea (short-form, long-form, live)
- [ ] Plan repurposing: one long video → multiple short clips

### Promotion Plan

- [ ] Define video purpose (awareness, traffic, engagement, conversion)
- [ ] Identify audience and active platforms
- [ ] Upload natively to each platform (don't just share links)
- [ ] Add captions and subtitles
- [ ] Note how this supports overall goals
