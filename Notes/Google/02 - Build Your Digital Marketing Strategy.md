## Setting Suitable Goals (45 m)

### Goal Types

| Dimension | Type | Description |
|-----------|------|-------------|
| **Time horizon** | Short-term | Quick wins; next few months; immediate feedback |
| | Long-term | Broad objectives; a year or more; sustained growth |
| **Measurement** | Soft goals | Qualitative; customer sentiment, engagement |
| | Hard goals | Quantitative; e.g. sales +25% in Q3 vs previous year |
| **Focus** | Brand perception | Trust, reliability, awareness; harder to measure |
| | Customer acquisition | New customers, conversions; easier to measure |

### SMART Goals Framework

| Criterion | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Specific** | Clear and well-defined. Instead of "Increase website traffic" → "Increase website traffic by 15% in 3 months." |
| **Measurable** | Track progress effectively. You can verify whether the target has been reached. |
| **Attainable** | Realistic based on data, trends, and historical performance. |
| **Relevant** | Aligns with broader business objectives. Only focus on traffic if it supports overall growth. |
| **Time-bound** | Clear deadline (e.g., 3-month time frame). |

## Digital Presence Audit (25 m)

| Focus Area | What to Check |
|------------|---------------|
| **Gap identification** | Missing channels or incomplete information (e.g., outdated contact details) |
| **Findability** | How quickly and easily customers can locate you via search, AI assistants, and social media |
| **Conversion points** | Hurdles in online ordering or contact options |
| **Consistency** | Branding elements (logos, colours, fonts, tone) across all channels |
| **AI visibility** | Whether your business appears in AI-generated answers (Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Perplexity). Structured data, clear content, and authority help. |

### Audit Tools

| Tool | Use |
|------|-----|
| **GA4** | Traffic sources, user behaviour, conversion funnels |
| **Google Search Console** | Search performance, indexing issues, keyword impressions |
| **Social platform analytics** | Follower growth, engagement rates, content performance |
| **AI audit** | Search your brand in ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews to see what AI says about you |
| **Competitor benchmarking tools** | SEMrush, Ahrefs, SimilarWeb for competitive positioning |

## Research the Competition (25 m)

### What to Analyse

| Area | Questions |
|------|-----------|
| **Mission and values** | Sustainability, innovation, community? How do they build brand loyalty? |
| **Unique selling points (USPs)** | Speed, low prices, service? What makes them stand out? |
| **Price positioning** | Premium or budget-conscious? How does this inform your positioning? |
| **Customer perception** | Reviews, testimonials, social media—how do customers really view them? |
| **AI and automation** | Are they using chatbots, AI-powered personalisation, or automated campaigns? |

### Search & Social Research Questions

| Research type | Question |
|---------------|----------|
| **Top results** | Who ranks highest for your key search terms? Who appears in AI Overviews? |
| **Keywords** | What words and phrases do competitors use in page titles? |
| **Social media messaging** | How do they present their brand? Which platforms get the most engagement? |
| **Location** | How does adding your local area change search results? |

## Stand Out from the Competition (30 m)

### Unique Selling Points (USPs)

Your USP is a clear, concise statement highlighting your business's benefits and how you solve customer problems (e.g., faster delivery, eco-friendly products).

| Principle | Action |
|-----------|--------|
| **Speak in a human voice** | Be natural, relatable, and authentic. |
| **Promote your USP** | Adjust tone per channel; brief on social, expanded on website. |
| **Tailor to your audience** | Focus on your ideal customer. See [[03 - Know Your Digital Customers]]. |

### Mission and Values

Your mission and core values define your business purpose. Aligning them with customer needs enhances loyalty.

| Element | Description | Example |
|---------|-------------|---------|
| **Purpose** | Primary goal | "To inspire healthier communities" |
| **Values** | 3–5 core values | Innovation, Sustainability, Community, Inclusivity, Transparency, Customer-centricity, Integrity |
| **Mission statement** | Purpose + values combined | "To inspire healthier communities through sustainable and inclusive wellness solutions." |

### SWOT Analysis

| Factor | Questions |
|--------|-----------|
| **Strengths** | What does your business excel at? (customer service, products, processes) |
| **Weaknesses** | What could be improved? (resources, technology) |
| **Opportunities** | What external factors can you capitalise on? (trends, AI tools, underserved segments) |
| **Threats** | What could negatively affect you? (competitors, economy, AI disruption, customer preferences) |

## Checklists

### SMART Checklist

- [ ] **Specific:** What specific area needs improvement? What do you want to achieve?
- [ ] **Measurable:** How will you measure success? What metrics show progress?
- [ ] **Achievable:** Is this realistic given current resources and past performance?
- [ ] **Relevant:** How does this support overall business objectives?
- [ ] **Time-bound:** When do you want to achieve this? What deadline keeps you on track?

### Strategy Checklist

- [ ] Set specific, measurable goals to give your strategy clear direction.
- [ ] Craft your mission statement that reflects your business's purpose and values.
- [ ] Identify your unique selling point (USP) by highlighting what makes your business stand out.
- [ ] Use AI tools (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini) to help brainstorm and refine your strategy.

### Audit Checklist

- [ ] Identify key audit goals (e.g., improving search visibility, enhancing engagement).
- [ ] Ensure business information is accurate and consistent across all platforms.
- [ ] Test how easily customers can find you via search engines, AI assistants, and social media.
- [ ] Review how easy it is for customers to contact you or order online.
- [ ] Develop an action plan—prioritise updating outdated info or improving SEO.

### Research Checklist

- [ ] What is the USP of this brand?
- [ ] How do customers perceive this brand?
- [ ] Who are their main competitors?
- [ ] What AI tools or automation are they using?

### Stand Out Checklist

- [ ] Who is my target audience?
- [ ] Who are my competitors?
- [ ] What problems does my target audience have?
- [ ] How can I solve them?
- [ ] Write your business purpose in one sentence.
- [ ] List your core values.
- [ ] Combine purpose and values into a simple, impactful mission statement.
